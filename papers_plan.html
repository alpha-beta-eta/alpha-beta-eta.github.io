论文翻译计划

  4. A Structural Approach to Operational Semantics: 被认为是现代操作语义的先声
  5. SCHEME: An Interpreter for Extended Lambda Calculus: 最早的关于Scheme语言的设计的论文
  6. Definitional Interpreters for Higher-Order Programming Languages: 或许可以称为古典操作语义的总结
  7. Abstracting Abstract Machines: 通过抽象机器推导抽象解释
  8. A Formulae-as-Types Notion of Control: 探究经典逻辑的Curry-Howard对应
